Personality Temperament Survey

This survey helps assess your temperament of potential strengths and weaknesses. There are two options for the survey:

40 questions total that take about 15 minutes to complete
or
120 questions total that take about 45 minutes to complete

There are 4 sections. In each section you will find a series of descriptive words. Read each word or phrase and mark the number according to how well it describes the REAL you.
Each question is rated from 1 to 5:

  • 1 = Definitely NOT me
  • 2 = Usually NOT me
  • 3 = Usually me
  • 4 = Mostly me
  • 5 = Definitely me

It's important that you be honest and objective.
Don't answer according to how you want to be or don't want to be.
BE COMPLETELY 100% HONEST WITH YOURSELF...
BE RAW ABOUT WHO YOU ARE RIGHT NOW AT THIS POINT IN YOUR LIFE.
